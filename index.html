Privacy Policy for Edge Lighting

Edge Lighting ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information to our servers.

The app may access certain data locally on your device to provide functionality, including:

  • Images or media files selected by you for custom backgrounds
  • Device display information required to render edge lighting effects

All data is processed locally and is not uploaded or shared.

2. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Photos / Media Access – Used only when you choose images for customization.
  • Draw Over Other Apps – Used to display edge lighting effects over other applications.
  • Wallpaper Access – Used to set live wallpaper functionality.

These permissions are used strictly for app features and not for collecting personal data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Play Services. These services may collect limited technical data under their own policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. Since processing occurs locally, data exposure is minimized.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

8. Advertising

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymized information to provide relevant ads.
